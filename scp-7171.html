SCP-7171 – The Whispering Entity

By: Observer Ruzgar C.

SCP-7171 – Observer Report: Ruzgar C. Case Series

Item #: SCP-7171
Object Class: Keter-Persistent (currently proposed for reclassification to "Nullified")

Special Containment Procedures:
As of Incident-7171-25, SCP-7171 is considered non-manifest and inactive. Surveillance around former manifestation zones (Ruzgar C.’s residence and nearby wooded area) is to be maintained for at least 6 months post-final encounter. Any reappearance of black fluid residue or auditory anomalies must be reported immediately to MTF Psi-8 (“Dream Watchers”).

Description:
SCP-7171 is a humanoid entity composed of absolute light-absorbing material, approximately 2.5–3 meters in height. It appears in a black suit, lacks facial features, and moves silently. It leaves behind a thick, putrid-smelling fluid and influences dreams.

Observer Profile: Ruzgar C.
Age: 12
Location: Adana, Turkey
Known for exceptional psychological resistance to SCP-7171.

Key Incident Logs:
- Manifestations during 4:00 AM hours
- Unsolved deaths and missing persons
- Final defiance: “You can’t scare me.”

Status: Neutralized.
